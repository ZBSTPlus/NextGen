--- layout: grid_layout title: Find the Disarium Prime Numbers description: | In this game the Player must select all the disarium numbers exist in the given grid in the given time limit. rules: | Select all the disarium numbers which are displayed. A number if the sum of its power of the positions from left to right is equal to the number. Example: 11 + 32 + 53 = 1 + 9 + 125 = 135 You should not exceed more than 8 wrong answers. ---
